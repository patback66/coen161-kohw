What We Do

We offer a wide variety of options in out camps! From one to three week programs, you're kids will come home begging to retun.

Every day is a new adventure at KOWH Camps. Our extensive range of activity options offers campers incredible ways to grow and have fun. As campers take on new challenges and enjoy old favorites, they develop their skills and confidence, all while having the time of their lives.

Our program is built around our three fields – athletics, arts, and adventure. In addition, we have exclusive KOWH Camp activities created by our very own counselors. As you’ll see below, the broad range of activity choices offers something for every camper every day!
