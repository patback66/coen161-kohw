FAQ

Here are some of the common questions parents and campers have:

What is your admission policy?

KOHW Camps accepts all children who can participate in and benefit from our full in-camp and wilderness travel program regardless of race, color, religion or national origin.

Forum

Are scholarships available?

Financial aid is available to campers and families who would not otherwise be able to attend. Most aid is partial. For further information, email KOWH@gmail.com.

Forum

When may I visit my camper?

Parents are welcome to visit Songadeewin anytime after the first two weeks of their child’s stay. The best time to visit, however, is at mid-season on the last weekend of the first session, when there are special events for both parents and campers. We try to be as flexible as possible with parents’ schedules but we can’t change trip assignments to suit your visit. Please call ahead to be sure your child is not out on a trip!

Forum

Campers must be physically and emotionally prepared to fully participate in the programs that KOWH Camps offers. Songadeewin of Keewaydin accepts all children who meet the criteria for admission regardless of race, ethnicity, disability, religion, sexual orientation or national origin.
