Introduction A Little About Ourselves

Summer camp is a great opportunity for kids of all ages to experience a wide range of physical, educational, recreational and spiritual activities, all in an exciting, hands-on creative environment that is sure to be a winner! Campers will enjoy the chance to make new friends, create happy memories, and to grow and improve their skills in a variety of areas. With our outstanding program, friendly children and dedicated staff, they are sure to have an amazing summer here at KOWH Camps!

Activities Just A Sneak Peak.

Where to start? There are so many games, activities, crafts, theme-days, stories, and more that make summer camp special! Whether it’s an activity a camper already loves, or an opportunity to try and thrive at something new, sports for every age and experience level are part of each session. A cool-off in the pool is a daily stop for everyone. Swim lessons included for non-swimmers!

Jump In! Get Your Summer Started.

Our goal is to build self-esteem while teaching community living skills, respect for the environment, and a love for the outdoors. We work hard on cooperation, and don’t spend much time on competition, so there aren’t any cups or ribbons or medals. We believe effort and reward are related, and the reward is sweeter when it’s earned.
